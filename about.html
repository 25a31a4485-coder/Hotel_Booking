About us

EST. 1912 · 113 YEARS

Grand Hotel first opened its doors in 1912, making it 113 years old in 2025. What began as a seaside inn for travelers has grown into a 5‑acre (approx. 21,000 m²) cliffside estate, blending historic charm with modern luxury. The property includes the main building, private beach, gardens, and a marina.
